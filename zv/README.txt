A Pen created at CodePen.io. You can find this one at https://codepen.io/Zaku/pen/fyLKw.

 Color-Picker based on hsl-color.

- h: move left/right
- s: scroll up/down
- l: move up/down


- Click: lock color
- DoubleClick: release lock

Open pen with hash to convert color

- http://codepen.io/Zaku/pen/fyLKw#ff0000
- http://codepen.io/Zaku/pen/fyLKw#rgb(255,0,0)
- http://codepen.io/Zaku/pen/fyLKw#hsl(0,100,50)


The idea came to me after I had seen http://codepen.io/patrikhjelm/pen/ucKEz
